## How FIREGUARD Works

FireGuard is an intelligent fire prevention monitoring system designed to take action before risk turns into emergency. Here’s how it works:

![Installation](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/13114b_a4ed16a5391a4a499612c76d9a6fb85e~mv2.gif)

PROFESSIONAL

INSTALLATION

We send a certified technician to install the FireGuard device in the designated electrical panel of your facility.

Each component is configured according to the technical specifications of your site.

![Circuit Main](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/13114b_0e8f04cce4194cf1b98f239ba7408748~mv2.gif)

REAL-TIME

MONITORING 24/7

Once installed, our system continuously monitors critical electrical parameters in real time.

If values fall outside of safe thresholds, FireGuard immediately alerts the client via email and they will receive a phone call from us.

![Van Assistant](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/13114b_db4670eefcc043618f7fd06a5de04d76~mv2.gif)

EXPERT ASSISTANCE (IF NEEDED)

If the situation requires on-site action, we dispatch a certified technician to assess and resolve the issue directly at your location.

PEACE OF MIND

With FireGuard, you’re not just informed — you’re protected. Our proactive monitoring means fewer surprises and faster response times.
